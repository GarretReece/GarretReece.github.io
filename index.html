Recurring Classes and Socials

Social Dances

  • Every Tuesday at 9:00pm Austin City Dance Club hosts a social dance.
  • The second Friday of every month Go Dance North hosts the Code Blue social dance with a WCS side room.

Classes

  • Most Tuesdays starting at 6:15pm Austin City Dance Club has leveled group classes, with levels 1, 1.5, 2, 3, and 4. The level 1 class on the first Tuesday of the month is aimed at absolute beginners and is a great place to start.
  • Most Wednesdays at 9:00pm Chris Brown hosts a critique session at Austin Uptown Dance. Video recording and feedback from peers in a friendly setting. Organized on facebook at the group's Facebook page.
